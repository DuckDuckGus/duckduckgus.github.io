Profile
About

I'm a legal operations and client engagement professional based in Bilbao, Spain. I work at the intersection of process design, automation, and client experience — building the systems that allow legal teams to operate efficiently and focus on what matters: serving clients.

At my current firm, I hold three simultaneous roles spanning social media management, client engagement consulting, and legal operations automation. I've built automated data pipelines, intake workflows, and client-facing portals — all as a one-person operations team using tools like Zapier, Google Apps Script, and Google Data Studio.

I hold a Master in Business Diplomacy from the University of Deusto and bring native-level English and Filipino, C1 Spanish and French, and a sharp editorial instinct to everything I do.
