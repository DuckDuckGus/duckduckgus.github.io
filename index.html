Profile
About

I'm a legal operations and client experience consultant based in Bilbao, Spain. I work with businesses to design and automate the critical processes and systems that power their most important work — so high-level professionals can focus on strategic, client-facing priorities.

My consulting approach spans the full engagement lifecycle:

  • Designing frictionless client intake and onboarding workflows
  • Automating data pipelines for seamless knowledge management
  • Developing self-service portals and resources for always-on client support
  • Measuring and optimizing the client journey for maximum impact

I bring a versatile automation toolkit to every engagement, with deep expertise spanning API integrations (Zapier), custom scripts (Google Apps Script, Python), data wrangling (SQL, Excel), and analytics (Google Data Studio). But I'm equally adept with no-code tools like Airtable, Notion, and Squarespace — and I'm a quick study when it comes to picking up new platforms.

I hold a Master in Business Diplomacy from the University of Deusto and bring the cultural fluency of a native English and Filipino speaker with professional proficiency in Spanish and French.

Above all, I bring a keen eye for identifying opportunities and inefficiencies. I excel at recognizing manual, repetitive tasks that create bottlenecks and transforming them into streamlined, automated workflows that drive meaningful business improvement.
